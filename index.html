The Event

One Nation.
One Community.

Get Involved

Three Ways
to Participate

Whether you're in DC or across the country, there's a place for your chapter in this event.

🏟️

Come to DC

Head to Franklin Hall in Washington DC for the main event — watch the match and be part of a live ArsenalVision Podcast recording featuring Jacob from Poorly Drawn Arsenal.

  • Live match viewing at Franklin Hall
  • Live AVP podcast recording on the day
  • Including Jacob from Poorly Drawn Arsenal
  • Chance to win club-level game tickets
🍺

Watch With Your Chapter

Head to your local Arsenal America chapter pub and watch the game with your people. At halftime, tune into our exclusive livestream and be part of the show from wherever you are.

  • Watch at your local chapter pub
  • Tune into the halftime livestream
  • Chapter shout-outs on air
  • Featured in post-event podcast episode

More Than a Match

Supporting the
Arsenal Foundation

This event isn't just about football — it's about giving back. Arsenal Across America is proud to support the ArsenalVision Podcast and Arseblog fundraiser for the Arsenal Foundation. Donations support the Foundation's work in the Za'atari refugee camp on the Syrian border, where — in partnership with Save the Children — the Coaching for Life programme uses football to give boys and girls a safe space to develop friendships and life skills, stay in school, and build toward a brighter future.
